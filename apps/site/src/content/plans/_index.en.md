This section records current project status and the next development steps. Plans describe work that is proposed or awaiting validation; they do not imply that a feature is already implemented or publicly released.

Project pages present completed work. Development plans instead explain what will change next, why it matters, how priorities are ordered, and which observations support those decisions.
