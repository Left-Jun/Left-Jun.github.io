## Overview

Asherah's Lament combines platforming, resource management, and multi-ending progression. It was also a high-intensity practice in team collaboration and rapid development.

## My role

- Served as team lead, main programmer, gameplay designer, and music producer.
- Coordinated the basic control system, gameplay loop, and overall rhythm.
- Helped integrate the structure of underground collection, surface climbing, and ending branches into a complete experience.

## Technical implementation

- Implemented movement, jumping, dashing, wall detection, respawn, and dual-state switching.
- Designed and implemented spirit value, safety time, collection logic, and multi-ending checks.
- Developed shortcut construction, area-based timers, and asynchronous scene loading.
